Italian Lasagna

Lasagna... Widely known as one of the best dishes to ever come out of Italy! Here you will learn how to make this deliciousness right at home!

Ingredients:

  • Meat: Make sure that you have sweet italian sausage and lean ground beef;
  • 1 large onion;
  • 3 Gloves of garlic
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes;
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce;
  • Tomato paste;
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 50g of fresh parlsey;
  • 50g of dried basil leaves;
  • 1 teaspoon of salt;
  • Italian seasoning;
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper;
  • Lasagna pasta sheets;
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella and ricotta;
  • 1 egg.

Instructions:

  1. Make the meat sauce;
  2. Cook the noodles until it is al dente;
  3. Mix the ricotta and the egg;
  4. Layer the lasgna according to the instructions down below;
  5. Cover the tray with foil and put on a pre-heated oven;
  6. Allow the lasagna to rest once it leaves the oven.

How to layer the lasagna:

Make sure you follow this exact order!

  1. Meat sauce;
  2. Pasta sheets;
  3. Ricotta mixture;
  4. Mozzarella slices;
  5. Meat sauce;
  6. Parmesan cheese;
  7. Repeat the layers and then top with the remaining parmesan.

How long should it cook:

Once you have assembled your lasagna make sure to put it in a pre-heated oven for no more than 50 minutires.

For the first 25 minutes make sure that it is covered with foil, once that is out of the way, remove the foil and let it cook until you reach the 50 minutes. Make sure you have a timer with you!!

After all is said and done, allow the lasagna to rest for at least 15 minutes before you dig in. Enjoy!
