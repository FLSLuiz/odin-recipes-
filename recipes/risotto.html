Italian Mushroom Risotto

Again with a banger of a dish straight out of Italy! Learn how to make a creamy Mushroom Risotto and surprise yourself, your friends and family!

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups of chicken broth (or as needed);
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 pound of mushrooms, whichever you want, but make sure they are thin;
  • 2 thinly sliced medium shallots (or one large);
  • 1 & 1/2 cups of risotto rice;
  • 1/2 cup of a dry white wine;
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter;
  • 3 tablespoons of finely chopped chives;
  • 1/2 cup of grated parmesan cheeese;
  • Salt and black pepper to taste.

Directions:

  1. Warm the chicken broth in a saucepan over low heat;
  2. While you wait, warm 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large sauce pan over medium heat. Throw in the mushrooms and stir until soft;
  3. Once they are cooked, should not take longer than a couple minutes, remove the mushrooms and their liquid and set them asice;
  4. Add the rest of the olive oil in the saucepan and stir the shallots for a minute;
  5. Add the rice and cook it until it is coated with oil and looks golden, 2 minutes should do the trick;
  6. Pour in the wine and stir constantly until it is fully abosrved;
  7. Add 1/2 cup of warm chicken broth and stir constantly, you want to this a few times until the rice is tender, 15/20 minuties is enough;
  8. Remove from heat and add the mushrooms and their liquid you set aside earlier;
  9. Add the butter, chives, parmesan cheese and do not forget to season the risotto with salt and pepper;
  10. Serve immediately and enjoy!
