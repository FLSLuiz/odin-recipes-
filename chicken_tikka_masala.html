Chicken Tikka Masala

This known dish can be done at home and rival any Indian restaurant!

Make it at home and test it yourself! It is incredibly aromatic and tasty, definitely something you will want to do again!

Ingredients:

Ingredients for the chicken marinade:

  • 800g of boneless and skinless chicken;
  • 125g of greek yogurt;
  • 3 gloves of garlic;
  • 1 tablespoon of ginger;
  • 2 teaspoons of garam masala;
  • 1 teaspoon of turmeric;
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin;
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground red chili powder;
  • 1 teaspoon of salt;

For the sauce:

  • 2 tablespoos of vegetable oil;
  • 2 tablespoons of butter;
  • 3 gloves of finely chopped carlic;
  • 1 large onion;
  • 1 tablespoons of grated ginger;
  • 1 & 1/2 teaspoons of garam masala;
  • 1 & 1/2 teaspoons of ground cumin;
  • 1 teaspoons of turmeric powder;
  • 1 teaspoons of ground coriander;
  • 350g of tomato sauce;
  • 1 teaspoon of ground red chili powder;
  • 1 teaspoon of salt;
  • 1 cup of thickening cream;
  • 1 teaspoon of brown sugar;
  • 1/4 cup of water if necessary;
  • 50g of fresh chopped cilantro;

Instructions:

  1. Pick a medium sized bowl and combine the chicken with all the indredients listed for the marinade. let it rest for a minimum of 15 minutes, overnight for best results;
  2. Pour some vetagle oil in a large, heated pan, once it sizzles, slowly add the chicken pieces and fry until browned. Should take 3 minutes for each side, once it is done set it aside and keep it warm;
  3. Melt the butter in the same pan. Fry the onion for 4 minutes and make sure to scrape the browned bits that are stuck at the bottom;
  4. Add your galic and ginger and saut√© for a minute or so and then add your garam, masala, turmeric, cumin and cilandtro. Fry and stir for no more than 30 seconds;
  5. Pour in the tomato sauce, chili powders and salt and let it simmer for no more than 15 minutes;
  6. Stir the heavy cream and sugar through the sauce and then add the chicken and all its juices for an extra 8 minutes until is thoroughly cooked. If needed add some water to thin out the sauce;
  7. Garnish with the remainder of your cilantro and serve with garlic Naan bread or white rice!
